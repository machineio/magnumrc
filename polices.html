    Additional Drivers

    You may have up to one additional drivers free of charge, whose names and signatures must be placed on the rental agreement.

    Mileage and Gasoline

    Unlimited Mileage is included in all of our rentals. Our gas policy is that you simply return the car with the gas tank at the same level as when you received it. Gas prices are about the same in all Costa Rica, with two types of gasoline to choose from, Regular and Premium. All of our cars use the cheaper Regular Gasoline. The cost per litter is about 610 colones (about $1.22 USD based on $498 exchange rate), which equals approximately $4.64 USD per gallon as of March 25, 2011. See current prices here: http://www.recope.go.cr/info_clientes/precios_productos/ See current USD currency exchange rates from Banco Central de Costa Rica here: http://www.bccr.fi.cr/flat/bccr_flat.htm

    Driving Restrictions

    You may drive our vehicles within Costa Rica without restrictions. Unfortunately, you cannot visit our neighboring countries of Nicaragua and Panama driving our cars.

    PICK-UP AND DROPOFF

    Hours of Operation & Airport Fees Pick up and drop off service for arrival and departure is available free of charge at San Jose (SJO) International Airport. We offer this service 24 hrs, 7 days per week. So as part of our personalized service to you, no matter what time your arrival or departure, we can be there to pick you up and drop you off.

    Not arriving by air?

    If you require to be picked up somewhere different than from the airport, just mention where you would like us to meet you in the comments section of the reservation form, and we will meet you at the time and place of your choice.

    Pick up or drop off in another City

    If you choose to return your rented vehicle in another city then please see our drop off fee table.

    INSURANCE

    Our Insurance Coverage includes:

    Primary Liability Insurance (PLI) is included in basic rental price.. PLI covers Ambulance Service, Doctors, Hospitalization and Nurses for third parties with a maximum limit of $400,000 USD per accident or 100,000 USD per person. The coverage is only effective in Costa Rica. Collision Damage Waiver-Theft Protection (CDW-TP) CDW-TP is mandatory and must be purchased unless the renter's credit card has CDW-TP coverage. Credit card coverage is only valid for CDW-TP. If customer declines CDW-TP, then PLI becomes mandatory and must be purchased. Proof of credit card coverage is required at time of rental. Deductible Protection (DP) is optional and covers the CDW-TP and PLI deductible, but it does not cover theft of personal belongings.

    Deductible

    CDW has a deductible of $800, TP has a deductible of $2000 and PLI has a deductible is 20% of total damages to the third party property.
