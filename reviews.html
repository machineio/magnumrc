Scott and Michelle on March 6, 2014

/ In Property, Life style

Thank you!

I book a rental with Mareks way back in Sept/Oct 2013 for our vacation in Feb 2014. The rental was for CRV/RAV4. Even took advantage of the discount on payment for paying early. Now about two or three weeks before we were to depart on our vacation I get an email from Mareks asking if we would consider a two door. I said sure as there was only the wife and I. Never gave it any further thought. About a week later I emailed Mareks and asked what type of connection the stereo had so we could listen to the music on our phones. He replied that it had a cassette tape player and no AUX port or USB! So right then and there I knew we were getting stuck with a 20 year old car. He sent pictures and I almost had a heart attack. Was an at least 20 year old GMC tracker! Had to use a broom handle to hold the back window up to load and unload. Had to be the most uncomfortable ride I have been in since I can not remember when. I did go to the car rental places by the airport but to get a rental last minute for 19 days was going to be almost $2000.00 so we decided to lump it and take the 20 year old tracker. Now we were not just going to one location, staying and then going home, This vacation was an adventure to see a LOT of Costa Rica. We started off by going to Manzanillo on southern Caribbean side back across down to Puerto Jimenez and further on Osa peninsula, back up the coast and then through the interior to La Fortuna and our final destinations were Flamingo and Playa del Coco in northwest. We struggled along as it was unsafe to go more than 50 or 55 MPH in the tracker. We were putting up with the uncomfortable ride by making frequent stops. The last straw was on the drive from Manuel Antonio to La Fortuna. This 220KM drive took us 6 1/2 hours. The car almost over heated multiple times. We stayed behind a semi doing about 10KM as was afraid that if I passed it would blow the motor and it just did not have the power to pass. Once we arrived in La Fortuna I said to the wife I was done with that car and she agreed! I walked down to Alamo and rented an RAV4. I contacted Mareks and told him that we no longer feel safe driving the car and advised where we left it in La Fortuna. So to say I was not impressed with MrMagnum and Mareks was an understatement. Now the good part. The customer service from Mareks was first class. The day we were having the car delivered I expressed how unhappy I was and he offered to cancel the contract and refund all the money. I said no, we were kind of stuck and would take the vehicle. Mareks did refund for the unused days! This was going above and beyond and I thank him very much for this. So just because of his amazing customer service, I will 99% rent from him again! As long as he does not try and give me a 20 year old GMC tracker! Mareks, thanks for your amazing customer service!
